# LegalHub Backend

**LegalHub** is an innovative legal assistance platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and legal services through technology. By simplifying legal jargon and providing accessible legal resources, LegalHub empowers users to understand their rights, connect with legal professionals, and report cases with ease.

## üåü Overview

LegalHub is a comprehensive legal technology platform that democratizes access to legal information and services. The platform leverages AI-powered conversational interfaces to make legal knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of their legal background.

## üéØ Key Features

### 1. **AI-Powered Legal Chatbot**
- **Plain Language Explanations**: Get answers to legal queries in simple, everyday language without complex legal jargon
- **Multilingual Support**: Ask questions and receive responses in your preferred language
- **Context-Aware Responses**: The chatbot understands context and provides relevant, personalized legal information
- **24/7 Availability**: Access legal guidance anytime, anywhere

### 2. **Lawyer Booking System**
- **Browse Lawyers**: Search and filter lawyers by specialization, location, rating, and availability
- **Profile Details**: View comprehensive lawyer profiles including experience, success rate, and client reviews
- **Easy Scheduling**: Book consultations directly through the platform
- **Secure Communication**: Communicate with lawyers through encrypted messaging

### 3. **Legal Articles & Knowledge Base**
- **User-Generated Content**: Users can write and share legal experiences, tips, and insights
- **Lawyer Contributions**: Legal professionals can publish articles, guides, and legal updates
- **Community Engagement**: Comment, like, and discuss articles with other users
- **Search & Filter**: Find relevant articles by category, topic, or author

### 4. **Anonymous & Identified Case Reporting**
- **Flexible Reporting Options**: 
  - Report cases anonymously for sensitive matters
  - Report with full identity for cases requiring legal action
- **Secure Data Handling**: All case reports are encrypted and handled with strict confidentiality
- **Case Tracking**: Monitor the status and progress of reported cases
- **Evidence Upload**: Attach documents, images, and other supporting materials

### 5. **Analytics Dashboard (NGO/Government Organizations)**
- **Case Analytics**: 
  - View reported cases by category, location, and severity
  - Identify trending legal issues in specific communities
  - Track case resolution rates and timelines
- **Community Insights**: 
  - Analyze legal challenges faced by different demographics
  - Identify underserved communities requiring legal aid
- **Data-Driven Decision Making**:
  - Plan targeted sensitization campaigns based on case data
  - Allocate resources to areas with highest need
  - Measure impact of legal aid initiatives
- **Interactive Reports**: Generate custom reports with visualizations and export capabilities

## üë• User Roles

### Citizens/Users
- Ask legal questions via chatbot
- Book consultations with lawyers
- Read and write legal articles
- Report cases (anonymously or with identity)
- Track their cases and consultations

### Lawyers
- Manage their profile and availability
- Accept/decline consultation requests
- Write legal articles and guides
- Respond to user inquiries (optional)
- Build their reputation through the platform

### NGOs/Government Organizations
- Access comprehensive analytics dashboard
- View reported cases (with privacy protections)
- Monitor legal trends and patterns
- Plan intervention strategies
- Coordinate legal aid programs
- Generate reports for policy making
